Entrance Counseling: A federal requirement for all Federal Direct Loan recipients, Entrance Counseling provides you with information about your rights and responsibilities as a borrower, interest rates, repayment plans, and more. To complete Entrance Counseling and log into studentaid.gov, you will need your Federal Student Aid ID (FSA ID). Once you have logged in, select “Complete Loan Counseling” and choose the option for “Entrance Counseling”. If you do not have your FSA ID, instructions will be provided on how to create one. Federal Loan Master Promissory Note (MPN): The MPN is a legal document in which you promise to repay your loan(s) and any accrued interest and fees. To complete your MPN, use the instructions above to log into studentaid.gov, select “Complete Loan Agreement for a Subsidized/Unsubsidized Loan (MPN)”.
